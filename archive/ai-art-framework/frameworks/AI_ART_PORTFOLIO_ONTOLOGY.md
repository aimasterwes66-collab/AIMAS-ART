### Double Coin System (::)
Weight values from 0.1 to 1.0:
- ::0.1 = Minimal influence
- ::0.3 = Low influence
- ::0.5 = Moderate influence
- ::0.7 = High influence
- ::0.9 = Very high influence
- ::1.0 = Maximum influence (default when no weight specified)

### Nested Modifier Structure
Main Category > Subcategory > Specific Modifier :: Weight

Example:
"Character" > "Portrait" > "Close-up view" :: 0.8
